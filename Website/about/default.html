It's all about cards, colors, and chance.


I'm a software developer who happens to live board games. Oddly enough, I like to make computer games out of board games more than I like to actually play them.

NOTE: If you don't know much about computer programming or computer science the rest of this about is not going to make a lick of sense.

As with most side programming projects, there isn't enough time in the day so only the best of projects every actually get done. I started writing Coloretta several years ago and have stopped, started, restarted, stopped, and then restarted work in it. It was until two months ago that I wanted to try writing a card based came that used a completely immutable design pattern. I plan on writing a blog about the advantages and disadvantages however in summary I think it has been a big help in both debugging AND allowing me to write the entire library in just a few weeks.

I'm putting this game out on the web hoping it'll bring some enjoyment to people. I've put a Donate link on just about every page. Anything I get will be put towards the cost of future webhosting and will not be used to compensate myself.

Let me know what you think of the game!

Happy Coloretta-ing!
