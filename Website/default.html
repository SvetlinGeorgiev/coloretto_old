If you are looking for a simple yet enjoyable card game to spend countless hours on then you are at the right place!
While I'd love to take the credit for such a wonderful game I've gotta give credit where credit is due. Coloretta is a computer game I wrote based on "Coloretto". "Coloretto" is a card game designed by Michael Schacht and costs around $10.00. Your local board game store should have a copy.
